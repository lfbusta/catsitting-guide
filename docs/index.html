House Rules

  • Please don't smoke inside.
  • Feel free to invite friends over. The walls are super thick so you probably won't bother the neighbours.
  • As long as nothing is damaged and things are cleaned we are perfectly happy.
  • Make sure the cats always have access to both litter boxes i.e. don't leave the door to the bathroom closed. It's ok to throw Mochi in the bedroom while you're cooking/eating though. Just let her out once you're done.

Doors and Keys

  • The building, apartment, and trash room doors all unlock with the same key.
  • Our front door closes by itself so make sure you always have the key with you when you go out.
  • We have two spare keys in case things go wrong: A neighbour in the same building, and a friend who lives nearby. We'll give you their contact details over whatsapp.

Activity and leisure equipment

  • We'll share the wifi with you over whatsapp.
  • We have an apple TV with Netflix and Disney plus. You'll have to use the Apple TV remote to control it. It's a terrible remote, sry.

Bed Room

  • We will leave empty spaces for you in the wardrobe (right door).
  • Make sure the window is closed at all times to avoid the cats getting out. You can leave it open at the top for ventilation if you want to.

Living/Dinning Room

  • There's a yellow bird on the wall. It monitors the CO2 levels of the apartment. If it dies, it might be a good idea to open a window. It's not urgent though, and you won't die even if the bird stays dead.
  • Feel free to use the desk. It's really easy to destroy things when moving the desk from the standing to the sitting positions, so we'll leave it down and locked.

Balcony

  • Balconny is netted so the cats can't go into the neighbor's balcony.
  • The cats are allowed to go out under supervision.

Heating

  • The apartment stays at about 19ºC with the heating off in the winter so we usually don’t turn on the heating. But if you want you can turn it on by rotating the dial next to the front door and in the bedroom next to the door.
  • We have underfloor heating so it might take up to 8 hours for the apartment to heat up, so there is no point switching it off at night.

Cleaning

  • We recommend washing the sheets and duvet cover at least every other week. If you wash them in the morning they should be dry by the time you go to sleep at night.
  • Please use the squeegee (hanging in the shower) to wipe water off the glass panes after showering. Berlin has very hard water so calcium deposits build up fast if you don’t do this.
  • If you see that water in the shower is not draining well, please clean the filter of the shower drain. You might have to do this every 2-3 weeks.
  • Please vacuum the apartment because it gets very dusty with cat fur quite quickly. You can use the vacuum cleaner (next to the washing machine) or the Roomba (on the washing machine). When using the roomba, please make sure to empty it and remove hair and dust from the moving parts. The roomba cannot handle the rugs, so you'll need the vacuum cleaner for that.
  • There's a feather duster and a rug to dust surfaces on the white kallax on top of the washing machine.
  • Cleaning supplies for the kitchen are found in the kitchen cupboard under the sink.
  • Cleaning supplies for the bathroom and toilet are found in the bathroom cupboard.
  • Dishwasher soap, and other kitchen cleaning supplies can be found in the cupboard under the sink.
  • Washing machine soap is kept in a container on top of the washing machine.

Trash and Recycling

  • Garbage bags are all kept in the cupboard under the sink.
  • The trash cans in the apartment are color coded: red takes plastic and composite packaging, and grey is for other rubbish. There is a paper bin lined with a tote bag we use for paper trash, and a special bio bag for food waste (it’s green- we’ll leave one out for you). The cat litter goes in the green bin next to the washing machine. It’s generally good to double bag this as the litter gets quite heavy.
  • Garbage can be taken out to the main apartment building garbage room, which is accessible through the basement. The directions are: - Go to -1 floor with the elevator - Exit the elevator and go to the car park (door on your left) - Walk straight from the car park entrance to the other side of the room. On your left enter the door labeled “Bossestrasse 6a” - Go up the stairs to the ground floor - Exit to the inner garden of the apartment block - Walk right for a few steps. There will be a door on your right which you need your key to enter. The rubbish bins are also color coded, and you can check the pictures they have to see what goes where. Yellow is for plastics and packaging, black is for general rubbish, brown for food waste, and blue is generally for paper. Cat litter goes in the black bin.

She prefers to be the one approaching you, not the other way round :). She’s really playful as well and is much better behaved than Mochi when it comes to food! But she is quite fussy with the litter box. If it’s not clean she might not use it!

Playful, SMART, and food obsessed. She WILL get into any food you leave accessible to her. Even if its inside your bag. Even food left in plastic bags is not safe. We recommend to put Mochi in the bedroom when you’re cooking or even eating. Please don’t let her eat any of your food either, we are trying to train her to not expect food from people. Also, be careful when she gets very playful and starts rolling around on the floor. If you pet her she can play very rough and bites hard. Just disengage and play with her using a toy instead. Despite her quirks, she’s a very loving cat and loves cuddles and attention. She likes to get pets on your lap, and when she’s in a good mood she’ll often go to the kitchen and start rolling around the floor. This means she’s happy and wants to get a bit of attention! Proceed with caution.

Food

  • Gets easily nervous when others are nearby when she eats. Try to leave her alone while she does.
  • Likes the large gray food puzzle for her dry food.
  • Eats by the front door.
  • 80g of wet food per meal.

  • Food (both wet and dry) is found in the cupboard above the freezer in the kitchen.
  • They eat wet food twice a day (once when we have lunch and once before we go to sleep) and dry snacks once a day (when we have dinner).

Toilet & Grooming

  • Both cats use the litter tray in the bathroom and the tray in the living room.

  • We use clumping litter and throw the clumps into the green bin near the washing machine which we take out when full. It’s really important to keep on top of the box cleaning because otherwise Maki might pee somewhere else! She is very sensitive to a dirty box.
